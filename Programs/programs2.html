Sustainability

sus·tain·a·bil·i·ty
/səˌstānəˈbilədē/
noun
1. the ability to be maintained at a certain rate or level.
2. "the sustainability of economic growth"
avoidance of the depletion  of natural resources in order to maintain an ecological balance.
